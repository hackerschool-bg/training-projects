Round

About:

There are n circles: A1, A2...An. There is a path between two circles when they have two intersections. This program finds the shortest path from A1 to An.

Input:

First line needs to contain one number - the amount of circles

Next n lines should consist of three numbers - x, y and r:

x and y are the coordinates of the center of a circle

r is the radius of the circle

Then click submit

Example:

7
3 -9 4
-3 -5 2
0 0 7
0 6 3
4 6 3
5 7 4
8 9 1
