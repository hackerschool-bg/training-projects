Segments

n - the length of the line

a - the distance between the points George puts

b - the distance between the points Gergana puts

c - the distance that has to be between two points so that George and Gergana can paint it red

Result - the amount of segments which are not colored

Dots between segments ('.') represent placed dots, spaces (' ') - represent not placed dots. Symbol for equation ('=') in red represents a red-colored segment. Underscore ('_') represents a not-painted segment.
